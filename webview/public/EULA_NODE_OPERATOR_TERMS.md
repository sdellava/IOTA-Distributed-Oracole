## 18. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement is governed by and shall be construed in accordance with the laws of the Italian Republic, without regard to its conflict-of-law principles.

Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the competent courts of Milan, Italy.

## 19. Final Provisions

If any provision of this Agreement is held invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect. The invalid provision shall be replaced by a valid provision that comes closest to the economic purpose of the original provision.
